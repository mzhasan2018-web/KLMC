Who this is for

You may benefit if you:

  • Are struggling with weight, especially in your 30s–60s
  • Are experiencing perimenopause or menopause symptoms
  • Feel more tired, stressed, or out of balance
  • Have noticed changes in weight, sleep, or energy
  • Have tried different approaches without success
  • Women with PMOS struggling with weight or hormonal symptoms
  • Men experiencing low energy, weight gain or hormonal changes

Our approach

We take a GP-led, structured approach to:

  • Weight and metabolic health
  • Perimenopause and menopause-related changes
  • Energy, sleep, and stress

This includes:

  • Medical assessment (not just symptoms)
  • Lifestyle medicine (nutrition, activity, sleep)
  • Behaviour change support
  • A personalised plan tailored to your life
