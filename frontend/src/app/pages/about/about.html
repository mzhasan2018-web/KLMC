Background & training

Dr Uzair Shabbir completed his medical training at the University of Oradea before undertaking GP training in Ireland, where he now practises as a Specialist GP with a particular interest in lifestyle medicine, obesity medicine and metabolic health.

He holds Fellowship of the Irish College of General Practitioners (FICGP), Associate Fellowship of the Irish Institute of Pharmacy (AFIPC), and a Certificate in Lifestyle Medicine, alongside an ICGP/HSE Clinical Academic GP Fellowship.

Clinical philosophy

“Many patients know what they should do but need time, structure and support to make change realistic and sustainable.”

Dr Uzair’s approach is structured, compassionate and evidence-based — combining medical assessment with lifestyle medicine and behaviour change support, rather than brief, generic advice.
